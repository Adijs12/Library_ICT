Chetan Bhaga

Chetan Bhagat is a bestselling Indian author known for his relatable and engaging storytelling that captures the essence of modern Indian life. His novels, often set against the backdrop of India's middle-class society, explore themes of love, friendship, career struggles, and social issues. Bhagat's writing style is simple yet impactful, making his books accessible to a wide audience. Some of his most popular works, like "Five Point Someone," "2 States," and "The 3 Mistakes of My Life," have not only resonated with readers but have also been adapted into successful Bollywood films. His stories often inspire and provoke thought, reflecting the aspirations and challenges of contemporary India.
